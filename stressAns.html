Stress management

To get stress under control, try and find out what’s causing stress,
look for ways to reduce the amount of stress, and learn healthy ways to relieve stress.
Sometimes it’s clear where the stress is coming from, other times its not so clear.
Figuring out what causes stress is important to starting coping skills techniques that helps reduce stress or eliminate all together.
You will feel better if you find ways to get stress out of your system.

Steps toward managing stress

  1. Change your thinking
  2. When in conflict, negotiate
  3. Pace and balance yourself
  4. Mindfulness
  5. Mediations
  6. Relaxation (sport, music)
  7. Personal Counseling. (Therapy)
